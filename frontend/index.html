Clause 14.2 — Terms of Service Agreement

Original clause

“The User hereby agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Clarity and its affiliates from any and all claims, losses, liabilities, and legal expenses arising from use of the service...”

Plain English

If something tied to your use of the platform creates a dispute, you may end up paying the company’s legal costs and related losses.

What this could change for you

Financial exposure

This clause can shift major legal costs onto you.

Indefinite liability

The obligation can survive even after the agreement ends.

Next action

Ask for a liability cap, remove survival language where possible, and tie exposure to a defined dollar amount.
